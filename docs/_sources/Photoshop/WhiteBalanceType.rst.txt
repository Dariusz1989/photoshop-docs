================================================
WhiteBalanceType
================================================


Description
-----------



The lighting conditions (affects color balance).




Static Properties
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`ASSHOT<WhiteBalanceType.ASSHOT>` readonly           | Use the settings of the camera as shot.             |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`AUTO<WhiteBalanceType.AUTO>` readonly               | Automatically use the white balance settings.       |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`CLOUDY<WhiteBalanceType.CLOUDY>` readonly           | Use the settings as shot on a cloudy day.           |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`CUSTOM<WhiteBalanceType.CUSTOM>` readonly           | Use the custom settings for the shot parameters.    |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`DAYLIGHT<WhiteBalanceType.DAYLIGHT>` readonly       | Use the settings as shot in daylight.               |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`FLASH<WhiteBalanceType.FLASH>` readonly             | Use the settings as shot with a flash.              |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`FLUORESCENT<WhiteBalanceType.FLUORESCENT>` readonly | Use the settings as shot with fluorescent lighting. |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`SHADE<WhiteBalanceType.SHADE>` readonly             | Use the settings as shot in the shade.              |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`TUNGSTEN<WhiteBalanceType.TUNGSTEN>` readonly       | Use the settings as shot with tungsten lighting.    |
+-----------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+

================================================
GallerySecurityTextPositionType
================================================


Description
-----------



The position of the text displayed over gallery images as an antitheft deterrent.




Static Properties
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

+------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`CENTERED<GallerySecurityTextPositionType.CENTERED>` readonly     | Text is centered on each image.                  |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`LOWERLEFT<GallerySecurityTextPositionType.LOWERLEFT>` readonly   | Ltext is in the lower left corner of each image. |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`LOWERRIGHT<GallerySecurityTextPositionType.LOWERRIGHT>` readonly | Text is in the lower right corner of each image. |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`UPPERLEFT<GallerySecurityTextPositionType.UPPERLEFT>` readonly   | Text is in the upper left corner of each image.  |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`UPPERRIGHT<GallerySecurityTextPositionType.UPPERRIGHT>` readonly | Text is in the upper right corner of each image. |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------+

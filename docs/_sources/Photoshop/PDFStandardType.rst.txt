================================================
PDFStandardType
================================================


Description
-----------



The PDF/X standard with which the document complies.




Static Properties
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

+--------------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`NONE<PDFStandardType.NONE>` readonly             | The document does not use the PDF/X standard.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
+--------------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`PDFX1A2001<PDFStandardType.PDFX1A2001>` readonly | PDF/X-1a standard, which requires all fonts to be embedded, the appropriate PDF bounding boxes to be specified, and color to appear as CMYK, spot colors, or both; can be opened in Acrobat 4.0 and Acrobat Reader 4.0 and later.                                                                                                                             |
+--------------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`PDFX1A2003<PDFStandardType.PDFX1A2003>` readonly | PDF/X-1a standard, which requires all fonts to be embedded, the appropriate PDF bounding boxes to be specified, and color to appear as CMYK, spot colors, or both; can be opened in Acrobat 4.0 and Acrobat Reader 4.0 and later.                                                                                                                             |
+--------------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`PDFX32002<PDFStandardType.PDFX32002>` readonly   | PDF/X-3 standard, which requires all fonts to be embedded and the appropriate PDF bounding boxes to be specified, and for color allows either the use of color management and device-independent color (CIE L*a*b, ICC-based color spaces, CalRGB, and CalGray) or CMYK, spot colors, or both; can be opened in Acrobat 4.0 and Acrobat Reader 4.0 and later. |
+--------------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`PDFX32003<PDFStandardType.PDFX32003>` readonly   | PDF/X-3 standard, which requires all fonts to be embedded and the appropriate PDF bounding boxes to be specified, and for color allows either the use of color management and device-independent color (CIE L*a*b, ICC-based color spaces, CalRGB, and CalGray) or CMYK, spot colors, or both; can be opened in Acrobat 4.0 and Acrobat Reader 4.0 and later. |
+--------------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`PDFX42008<PDFStandardType.PDFX42008>` readonly   | PDF/X-4 standard, which requires all fonts to be embedded and the appropriate PDF bounding boxes to be specified, and for color allows either the use of color management and device-independent color (CIE L*a*b, ICC-based color spaces, CalRGB, and CalGray) or CMYK, spot colors, or both; can be opened in Acrobat 8.0 and Acrobat Reader 8.0 and later. |
+--------------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

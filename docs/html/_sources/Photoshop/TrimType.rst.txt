.. _TrimType:

================================================
TrimType
================================================


Description
-----------



The type of pixels to trim around an image.




Static Properties
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

+---------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`BOTTOMRIGHT<TrimType.BOTTOMRIGHT>` readonly | Removes from the image an area the color of the lower right pixel.                                              |
+---------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`TOPLEFT<TrimType.TOPLEFT>` readonly         | Removes from the image an area the color of the upper left pixel.                                               |
+---------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`TRANSPARENT<TrimType.TRANSPARENT>` readonly | Trims away transparency at the edges of the image, leaving the smallest image containing nontransparent pixels. |
+---------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

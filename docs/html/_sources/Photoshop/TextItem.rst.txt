TextItem
================================================


Description
-----------

The text object contained in an art layer.

| :ref:`justification<TextItem.justification>`                       | The paragraph justification.                                                                                                                                                                                           |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`kind<TextItem.kind>`                                         | The type of text.                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`language<TextItem.language>`                                 | The language.                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`leading<TextItem.leading>`                                   | The leading amount.                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`leftIndent<TextItem.leftIndent>`                             | The amount to indent text from the left. Range: -1296 to 1296.                                                                                                                                                         |

+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`maximumLetterScaling<TextItem.maximumLetterScaling>`         | The maximum amount (as a percentage) of space between letters. Range: 100 - 500; at 0, no space is added between letters. Valid only for justified text.                                                               |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`maximumWordScaling<TextItem.maximumWordScaling>`             | The maximum amount (as a percentage) of space between words (0 -1000; at 100, no additional space is added between words). Valid only for justified text.                                                              |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`minimumGlyphScaling<TextItem.minimumGlyphScaling>`           | The minimum amount (as a percentage) to scale the horizontal size of the text letters. Range: 50 - 200; at 100, the width of characters is not scaled. Valid only for justified text.                                  |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`minimumLetterScaling<TextItem.minimumLetterScaling>`         | The minimum amount of space (as a percentage) between letters. Range: 100 to 500; at 0, no space is added between letters. Valid only for justified text.                                                              |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`minimumWordScaling<TextItem.minimumWordScaling>`             | The minimum amount (as a percentage) of space between words. Range: 0 -1000; at 100, no additional space is added between words. Valid only for justified text.                                                        |

| :ref:`oldStyle<TextItem.oldStyle>`                                 | If true, old style is used.                                                                                                                                                                                            |

| :ref:`parent<TextItem.parent>` readonly                            | The object's container.                                                                                                                                                                                                |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

| :ref:`spaceBefore<TextItem.spaceBefore>`                           | The amount of space before each paragraph. Range: -1296 to 1296.                                                                                                                                                       |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`strikeThru<TextItem.strikeThru>`                             | The strike through option to use.                                                                                                                                                                                      |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`textComposer<TextItem.textComposer>`                         | The text composing engine to use.                                                                                                                                                                                      |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`tracking<TextItem.tracking>`                                 | The amount of uniform spacing between multiple characters. Range: -1000 to 10000.                                                                                                                                      |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`typename<TextItem.typename>` readonly                        | The class name of the object.                                                                                                                                                                                          |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`underline<TextItem.underline>`                               | Options for underlining the text.                                                                                                                                                                                      |

+--------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

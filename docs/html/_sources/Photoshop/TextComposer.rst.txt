================================================
TextComposer
================================================


Description
-----------



The text composer.




Static Properties
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

+---------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`ADOBEEVERYLINE<TextComposer.ADOBEEVERYLINE>` readonly   | Considers a network of break points for a range of lines and thus optimizes earlier lines in the paragraph to eliminate especially unattractive breaks later on. Results in more even spacing and fewer hyphens. |
+---------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`ADOBESINGLELINE<TextComposer.ADOBESINGLELINE>` readonly | Offers a traditional approach to composing type one line at a time. Useful if you prefer to have manual control over how lines break.                                                                            |
+---------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

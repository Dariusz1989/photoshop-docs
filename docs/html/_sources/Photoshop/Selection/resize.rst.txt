================================================
Selection.resize
================================================

   void **resize** (:ref:`number` **horizontal**, :ref:`number` **vertical**, :ref:`AnchorPosition` **anchor**)


Parameters
----------

+----------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **horizontal** | The amount to scale the selection horizontally (as a percentage). |
+----------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **vertical**   | The amount to scale the selection vertically (as a percentage).   |
+----------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **anchor**     | The point to scale around.                                        |
+----------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+



Description
-----------

Resizes the selected area to the specified dimensions and anchor position.

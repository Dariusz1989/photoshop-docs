.. _TiffEncodingType:

================================================
TiffEncodingType
================================================


Description
-----------



The encoding to use when saving to TIFF format.




Static Properties
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

+---------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`JPEG<TiffEncodingType.JPEG>` readonly       | JPEG compression, which is lossy and recommended for continuous-tone images, such as photographs.          |
+---------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`NONE<TiffEncodingType.NONE>` readonly       | No compression.                                                                                            |
+---------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`TIFFLZW<TiffEncodingType.TIFFLZW>` readonly | LZW compression, which is lossless and most useful for images with large areas of single color.            |
+---------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`TIFFZIP<TiffEncodingType.TIFFZIP>` readonly | Zip compression, which is lossless and most effective for images that contain large areas of single color. |
+---------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

.. _WarpStyle:

================================================
WarpStyle
================================================


Description
-----------



The warp style for text.




Static Properties
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`ARC<WarpStyle.ARC>` readonly               | The type is warped in the shape of an arc.                                                  |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`ARCH<WarpStyle.ARCH>` readonly             | Text is warped in the form of an arch.                                                      |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`ARCLOWER<WarpStyle.ARCLOWER>` readonly     | Warp is heavier on the lower or left edge of the text than on the upper or right edge.      |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`ARCUPPER<WarpStyle.ARCUPPER>` readonly     | Warp is heavier on the upper or right edge of the text than on the lower or left edge.      |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`BULGE<WarpStyle.BULGE>` readonly           | Text is warped outward on both the upper and lower or right and left edges.                 |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`FISH<WarpStyle.FISH>` readonly             | Text is warped in the shape of a fish.                                                      |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`FISHEYE<WarpStyle.FISHEYE>` readonly       | Text bulges in the middle and is squeezed on the edges as if viewed through a fisheye lens. |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`FLAG<WarpStyle.FLAG>` readonly             | Text is warped in the shape of a waving flag.                                               |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`INFLATE<WarpStyle.INFLATE>` readonly       | Text is inflated.                                                                           |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`NONE<WarpStyle.NONE>` readonly             | No warp.                                                                                    |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`RISE<WarpStyle.RISE>` readonly             | Text is warped in an undulating, rising pattern.                                            |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`SHELLLOWER<WarpStyle.SHELLLOWER>` readonly | Text is warped downward or to the right in the shape of a fan-like seashell.                |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`SHELLUPPER<WarpStyle.SHELLUPPER>` readonly | Text is warped upward or to the left in the shape of a fan-like seashell.                   |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`SQUEEZE<WarpStyle.SQUEEZE>` readonly       | Text is squeezed.                                                                           |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`TWIST<WarpStyle.TWIST>` readonly           | Text is twisted.                                                                            |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`WAVE<WarpStyle.WAVE>` readonly             | Text is warped in the shape of a wave.                                                      |
+--------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

.. _ArtLayer:

================================================
ArtLayer
================================================


Description
-----------

An object within a document that contains the visual elements of the image (equivalent to a layer in the Adobe Photoshop application).




Properties
^^^^^^^^^^

+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`fillOpacity<ArtLayer.fillOpacity>` readonly                         | The interior opacity of the layer. Range: 0.0 to 100.0.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`filterMaskDensity<ArtLayer.filterMaskDensity>` readonly             | The density of the filter mask (between 0.0 and 100.0)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`filterMaskFeather<ArtLayer.filterMaskFeather>` readonly             | The feather of the filter mask (between 0.0 and 250.0)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`grouped<ArtLayer.grouped>` readonly                                 | If true, the layer is grouped with the layer below.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`isBackgroundLayer<ArtLayer.isBackgroundLayer>` readonly             | If true, the layer is a background layer.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`kind<ArtLayer.kind>` readonly                                       | Sets the layer kind (such as 'text layer') for an empty layer. Valid only when the layer is empty and when 'is background layer ' is false. You can use the 'kind ' property to make a background layer a normal layer; however, to make a layer a background layer, you must set 'is background layer' to true. |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`layerMaskDensity<ArtLayer.layerMaskDensity>` readonly               | The density of the layer mask (between 0.0 and 100.0)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`layerMaskFeather<ArtLayer.layerMaskFeather>` readonly               | The feather of the layer mask (between 0.0 and 250.0)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`parent<ArtLayer.parent>` readonly                                   | The object's container.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`pixelsLocked<ArtLayer.pixelsLocked>` readonly                       | If true, the pixels in the layer's image cannot be edited.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`positionLocked<ArtLayer.positionLocked>` readonly                   | If true, the pixels in the layer's image cannot be moved within the layer.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`textItem<ArtLayer.textItem>` readonly                               | The text that is associated with the layer. Valid only when 'kind' is text layer.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`transparentPixelsLocked<ArtLayer.transparentPixelsLocked>` readonly | If true, editing is confined to the opaque portions of the layer.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`typename<ArtLayer.typename>` readonly                               | The class name of the object.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`vectorMaskDensity<ArtLayer.vectorMaskDensity>` readonly             | The density of the vector mask (between 0.0 and 100.0)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`vectorMaskFeather<ArtLayer.vectorMaskFeather>` readonly             | The feather of the vector mask (between 0.0 and 250.0)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

.. _Justification:

================================================
Justification
================================================


Description
-----------



The placement of paragraph text within the bounding box.




Static Properties
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

+----------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`CENTER<Justification.CENTER>` readonly                   | Text is centered on each line.                                    |
+----------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`CENTERJUSTIFIED<Justification.CENTERJUSTIFIED>` readonly | Justifies all lines except the last, which is center-aligned.     |
+----------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`FULLYJUSTIFIED<Justification.FULLYJUSTIFIED>` readonly   | Justifies all lines including the last, which is force-justified. |
+----------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`LEFT<Justification.LEFT>` readonly                       | Aligns the left edges, leaving the right edge ragged.             |
+----------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`LEFTJUSTIFIED<Justification.LEFTJUSTIFIED>` readonly     | Justifies all lines except the last, which is left-aligned.       |
+----------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`RIGHT<Justification.RIGHT>` readonly                     | Aligns the right edges, leaving the left edge ragged.             |
+----------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| :ref:`RIGHTJUSTIFIED<Justification.RIGHTJUSTIFIED>` readonly   | Justifies all lines except the last, which is right-aligned.      |
+----------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
